Dragon Tiger


Game Overview:
Dragon Tiger is one of the poker games. The object of the game is the banker drawn a card for dragon and Tiger to compete the point!

How to Win:
You can wager on whether the Tiger’s hand or the Dragon’s hand has the higher points of card.

Participation Instructions and Bet Options:
Select the amount you would like to wager and dragging it on the 'Dragon' 'Tiger' or 'Tie' sections of the gaming table.
The banker deals two cards. The two cards will be dealt face up, one to the Dragon first, then to the Tiger, the side with higher total wins.

Game Rule:
1. King is the highest point & A is the lowest point.
2. It competes the card points , the same point value is a "tie".
3. Dealer will deal the first card on Dragon’s hand position, and then the second card to Tiger’s hand position. The hand who has highest point will be the winner.

Payoff:
BET TYPES PAYOFF
Dragon’s Hand 1 ：1 (If the value of two cards are equal, you lose half of your bet.)
Tiger’s Hand 1 ：1 (If the value of two cards are equal, you lose half of your bet.)
Tie 1:8
